Add buyout tags to your forum-based shop.

There are 2 types of tags:

  • Spoiler title.
    Only items inside the spoiler are affected. All local tags are supported.
  • Local.
    The previous item is affected. There are two local tags: ~b/o which specifies buyout price and ~price which specifies a non-negotiable buyout price.

Currency format

Currency should be written as pair of positive number (decimal fractions are allowed) and currency name.

1.33 alch and 0.1 exa are valid currency records while -1 exa and 1 alch 1 exa aren't.

Here's the full list of supported currencies (plural forms are allowed as well):

  • Chromatic Orb — chrom
  • Orb of Alteration — alt
  • Jeweller's Orb — jewel
  • Orb of Chance — chance
  • Cartographer's Chisel — chisel, cartographer
  • Orb of Fusing — fuse, fusing
  • Orb of Alchemy — alch
  • Orb of Scouring — scour
  • Blessed Orb — blessed
  • Chaos Orb — chaos
  • Orb of Regret — regret
  • Regal Orb — regal
  • Gemcutter's Prism — gcp, gemcutter
  • Divine Orb — divine
  • Exalted Orb — exalted, exa
  • Mirror of Kalandra — mirror
  • Perandus Coin — perandus
  • Silver Coin — silver

Example

An example of a topic 
This is a very expensive item with buyout specified:
[item]
~b/o 500 exa

[spoiler="the extra content ~b/o 1 alch may be anywhere!"]
        Everything here is 1 alch!
        [item]
        .. but this item is slightly more expensive
        [item]
        ~b/o 1.1 alch

        [spoiler="~b/o 1.9 alch"]
                Nested spoilers are allowed as well. They will work just as you expect them to.
                [item]
        [/spoiler]

        [item]
        (^ this item is also 1 alch)
[/spoiler]
